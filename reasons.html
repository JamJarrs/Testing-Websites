Why is Josh?

Ah yes, why indeed? Well I believe it is for the following reasons

  • He never gives up, despite how much things get on top of him, he always eventually gets bak up and tries again
  • He always has a plan to get better even if plans in the past don't go exactly how we want them to

  • He has to be one of the most helpful people I know, anytime anyone he loves is in any kind of trouble he will drop everything to help
  • I have suspicions he may be a wizard

Regardless of how much he might protest, I think he's the best
